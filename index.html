About me

I am postdoc in the Kaleta-lab at the University Clinic Schleswig-Holstein, Kiel, Germany, and use metabolic modeling to investigate host-microbe interactions. Before that, I tried to understand how temperature and microbes change developmental decisions in the freshwater polyp Hydra - still a hidden passion of mine.
